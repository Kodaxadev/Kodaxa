## The problem

Many study products expose solved answers while skipping the first usable move a learner needed in order to begin.

## What was built

STEP is a local-only learning application with guided procedures, a structured MathLive work pad, learner-triggered checking, calculator practice, visual problem infrastructure, and friction-aware learning paths.

## Boundary

STEP is presented as a privacy-respecting learning instrument, not a grading platform. It has no account system or cloud-stored learner history by design.
