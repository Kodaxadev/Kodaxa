Agency Terminal is an operational design suite for The Agency // Lux Letifera: ticket intake, evidence ledger, group credit, appeals, officer workflows, security boundaries, hosting architecture, SQL schema, and a terminal-style website concept.

## Status boundary

It is presented here as a documented concept and architecture study rather than as a live deployed product.
